🛡 CYBER SECURITY AWARENESS CENTER

PHISHING ATTACKS

Phishing is one of the most common cyber attacks where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal sensitive information.

  • Never click suspicious email or SMS links
  • Verify website URLs before entering credentials
  • Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details
  • Check spelling errors and suspicious email addresses

MALWARE ATTACK

Malware includes viruses, ransomware, spyware, and trojans designed to damage systems or steal data.

  • Install software only from trusted sources
  • Use updated antivirus software
  • Avoid downloading cracked or pirated software
  • Keep operating systems and apps updated

PASSWORD SECURITY

Weak passwords allow attackers to easily access personal and corporate accounts.

  • Use strong passwords (12+ characters)
  • Use uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid using personal information in passwords
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

SOCIAL ENGINEERING

Attackers manipulate people psychologically to reveal confidential information.

  • Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages
  • Verify requests for sensitive data
  • Do not trust unknown callers claiming to be IT staff
  • Report suspicious communications immediately

PUBLIC WIFI RISKS

Public WiFi networks can allow attackers to intercept your internet traffic.

  • Avoid online banking on public WiFi
  • Use a VPN when accessing sensitive information
  • Disable automatic WiFi connections
  • Always log out of accounts after use

DATA BACKUP

Regular backups protect your data from ransomware or hardware failures.

  • Backup important files weekly
  • Use cloud storage or external drives
  • Keep backup devices disconnected when not in use
  • Test backups regularly

⚠ What To Do If You Suspect a Cyber Attack

  • Disconnect the affected device from the internet
  • Change passwords immediately
  • Inform your IT or security team
  • Scan the device with antivirus software
  • Report the incident using the Incident Reporting page
