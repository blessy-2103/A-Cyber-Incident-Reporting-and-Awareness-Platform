Phishing Attacks

Attackers impersonate trusted brands to trick you into clicking malicious links.

  • Never click suspicious email or SMS links
  • Verify URLs before entering credentials
  • Check for spelling and domain errors

Malware Defense

Software designed to steal data or lock systems via ransomware and trojans.

  • Install software from trusted sources only
  • Use verified antivirus protection
  • Keep your OS and apps fully updated

Password Hygiene

Your first line of defense is a strong, unique credential system.

  • Minimum length: 12+ characters
  • Mix symbols, numbers, and cases
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Social Engineering

Psychological manipulation aimed at leaking confidential business data.

  • Question urgent or threatening tones
  • Verify callers requesting sensitive info
  • Report suspicious messages immediately

Public WiFi Risks

Unsecured networks allow attackers to sniff and intercept your traffic.

  • Avoid banking on airport or cafe WiFi
  • Always use a VPN for company access
  • Disable "Auto-Connect" on your devices

Data Resilience

Protect your work from hardware failure or encryption-based attacks.

  • Automate weekly data backups
  • Use encrypted cloud storage solutions
  • Test your recovery process monthly

Emergency Protocol: Suspected Breach

  • 1. Disconnect device from Internet
  • 2. Immediately change local passwords
  • 3. Alert the CyberGuard IT Team
  • 4. Run a full Antivirus Deep Scan
  • 5. Log the event in the Reporting Page
